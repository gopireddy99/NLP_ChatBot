What is a computer?
A computer is a programming device that stores , retrieves , and processes data and gives output.

What is Data Science?
Data science is the interdisciplinary field. It helps in extracting insights or knowledge from data.

What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer or a robot controlled by a computer to do tasks that are usually done by humans because they require human intelligence and discrement.

What is text similarity?
Text similarity measures how much the meaning or content of two pieces of text are the same.
